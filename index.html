About the event

A private two-day gathering
for the people actually shipping AI.

Founders, operators, engineers, executives, and investors building with AI in the real world. Practitioner-led sessions, honest roundtables, private-villa networking, great food, open bar evenings, live DJs, and high-context conversations with people actually shipping AI.

What to expect

Two days. One villa. Zero filler.

A curated weekend of practitioner-led sessions, honest roundtables, and the kind of high-context networking that only happens when the right room shares a roof.

01

Practitioner sessions

Practitioner-led sessions from people doing the work — not panels of generalists. Real lessons from real builders, no recycled deck slides.

02

Roundtables

Founder, operator, and executive roundtables. Honest conversations, off the record, with the people deciding how AI gets shipped and adopted in their organisations.

03

Workshops

Hands-on workshops on building, scaling, deploying, and adopting AI in production. Bring problems, leave with answers.

04

Curated hospitality

Curated lunches, snacks, refreshments, and full hospitality across both days. Less queueing for coffee, more time talking to the right people.

05

Day 1 BBQ & DJ

Day 1 closes with live BBQ cooking, open bar, and a DJ set on the villa grounds. The format that turns acquaintances into actual contacts.

06

Day 2 evening mixer

Day 2 flows into an exclusive evening mixer with live DJ and open bar. High-quality conversations with the people who spent two days building together.

07

Private villa setting

A private villa designed to feel more like a retreat than a conference. No fluorescent lights, no name-tag lanyards, no convention-centre lobby.

08

Relaxation corner

A relaxation corner with games designed to bring people together between sessions. Sometimes the best intro is over a casual round of something, not a forced "what do you do?"
